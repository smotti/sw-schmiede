About

I received a Bachelor of Engineering degree, with focus on Information Technology and Distributed Systems. By now I have 4 years of experience in professional software development, system engineering and devops. Within those 4 years I’ve designed and build cloud-based and on-premise systems in the healthcare industry. Being in this industry also meant to have a strong focus towards quality and security.

I do embrace agile principles and practices, and highly value close cooperation with customers. I strive to build simple, long lasting and maintainable systems.

My current interest are: distributed systems, functional programming, devops and promoting professional engineering principles & practices within the software community.
